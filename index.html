Hi, I'm Szabolcs and this is my UX portfolio.

I love to design and build user-centered, easy-to-use digital products (websites and applications) which bring value to businesses.

I believe

“it is not enough that we build products that function, that are understandable and usable, we also need to build products that bring joy and excitement, pleasure and fun, and, yes, beauty to people’s lives.”

As Don Norman said.

I do:

Design

Design is how it works. It is about getting know the business and users, sketching out initial ideas, working on content, hierarchy, user flows and mixing colours and pushing pixels in Fireworks or Photoshop. It is Design with capital D.

I apply what we know about human behavior to interaction design to reach business and user goals.

Build

I make the design work in a web browser. My inner geek loves to code and be a developer, so I speak HTML / CSS and Javascript (jQuery).

I use Sass & Compass and commit code to Git. I can build working prototypes quickly.

Test

It is watching people use digital products, listening to what they say but more importantly what they do. It helps to find out users pain points and then remove that frustration.

It informs my decisions and helps me to iterate on my design and make it better.
